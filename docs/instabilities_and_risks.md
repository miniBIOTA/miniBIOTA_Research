# Instabilities and Risks

Known ecological instabilities and measurement gaps in the miniBIOTA biosphere.

---

## Current Instabilities

### Mesostoma ehrenbergii — Microcrustacean Predation Risk (Lake Biome)
- Hitchhiker introduced with wild Daphnia 2026-04-08. Eggs hatching in-system. Predatory on Daphnia, ostracods, and copepods. Outcome unresolved. Crayfish predation on Mesostoma is the hypothesized natural check, but not yet confirmed. 7 open loops tracking this arc.

### Ostracod Population Decline — Cause Unresolved (Lake Biome)
- Second ostracod introduction 2026-04-16 (hundreds, full acclimation). Surviving but reduced as of 2026-04-17. Two competing hypotheses: residual acclimation shock vs. active crayfish sediment predation. Population trajectory under observation.

### Ghost Shrimp Larval Recruitment — Outcome Unresolved (Lake Biome)
- Ghost shrimp have now reproduced in the lake biome, with zoea first documented on April 19, 2026. The unresolved question is whether the larvae can survive through the vulnerable stage between hatching and becoming juveniles. They remained visible for three nights and appeared to grow, but were no longer visible by May 1, 2026. Possible outcomes include predation, failed development, or detectability limits; juvenile recruitment is not confirmed.

### Sagittaria Root Consumption (Lake Biome)
- Crayfish observed digging beneath substrate to consume sagittaria roots 2026-04-17, causing progressive leaf death. Detritivore cascade is functioning, but long-term plant loss could reduce structural cover and oxygenation in the lake biome.

### Amber Snail Predation Pressure (Lakeshore Biome)
- 75 amber snails introduced 2026-04-17. Expected predation from mangrove tree crabs and hermit crabs once they resume feeding post-molt. Population sized to absorb losses, but establishment not yet confirmed.

### Shoreline Erosion
- Beach biome experiencing active sediment instability due to burrowing and water movement.

### Primary Producer Inefficiency
- Current plant assemblages are opportunistic and not optimized for structured trophic scaling.

### Food Web Complexity Without Standardization
- Species composition is historically experimental. Future direction trends toward simplification and known-role food web design.

## Measurement Status

### Currently Measured
- Temperature (terrestrial biomes and atmospheres) via SHT4x sensors.
- Humidity (terrestrial biomes and atmospheres) via SHT4x sensors.
- Coolant temperature in heat exchangers.

### Not Yet Measured
- CO2
- O2
- Methane
- Salinity drift
- pH
- Dissolved oxygen

Future instrumentation is planned. All unmeasured processes in cycle documentation are labeled as inferred.
